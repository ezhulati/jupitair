# Emergency Heating Repair in North Texas

When your heater fails on a freezing North Texas night, you need immediate help. Jupitair HVAC provides 24/7 emergency heating repair for furnaces, heat pumps, and all heating systems. Our owner-operated team responds fast to no-heat emergencies.

## Common Heating Problems We Fix

### Emergency Issues (Immediate Response)
- **No heat** - System won't produce warm air
- **Won't start** - Furnace doesn't turn on
- **Gas smell** - Dangerous gas leak situation
- **Carbon monoxide alarm** - CO detector activation
- **Electrical burning smell** - Overheating components

### Performance Problems
- **Insufficient heat** - House won't reach temperature
- **Cycling issues** - Frequent on/off cycles
- **Uneven heating** - Cold spots in home
- **High gas bills** - System running inefficiently
- **Strange noises** - Banging, whistling, rumbling

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: My heater is blowing cold air. What's wrong?**
A: Could be a failed ignitor, thermostat issue, or pilot light problem. Turn system off and call for service.

**Q: How quickly can you respond to no-heat emergencies?**
A: We typically arrive within 1-2 hours for heating emergencies, 24/7.

**Q: Do you repair all heating brands?**
A: Yes, we're certified for all major brands including Trane, Carrier, Lennox, Rheem, and Goodman.

**Q: What if my heat exchanger is cracked?**
A: This is a serious safety issue. We'll provide repair/replacement options, but the system must be shut down immediately.

**Q: Why does my heater smell when it first turns on?**
A: Dust burning off is normal for first use. Persistent smells need immediate attention.
